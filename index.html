Welcome to the Gallery of Bob Ross! This digital gallery celebrates the artistic genius of Bob Ross, bringing his iconic paintings to life through an interactive web experience. Explore the rich collection of episodes from "The Joy of Painting," delve into the vibrant colors used in each artwork, and enjoy high-quality images and video links that showcase Bob Ross's unique painting style. This website provides:

  • Detailed information about each episode, including titles, air dates, and links to YouTube videos of the episodes
  • A color palette feature showing the colors used in each painting, with the option to view these colors in detail
  • A user-friendly interface designed with Bootstrap 5 for a seamless browsing experience
  • Responsive design ensuring optimal viewing across various devices, from desktop to mobile
  • Minimalist and clean custom CSS that highlights Bob Ross's artwork and provides an enjoyable user experience
